Privacy Policy — KiCadReview

Effective July 2026

This app collects no data.

Your files stay on your device

KiCadReview opens KiCad project files from the locations you choose (the Files app, iCloud Drive folders you pick, or files shared to the app). The files are read on-device to display schematics and boards. They are never uploaded, transmitted, analyzed remotely, or shared with anyone. Markup you draw is saved to a sidecar file stored next to your project.

No tracking, no accounts, no analytics

The app has no account system, no analytics or crash-reporting SDKs, no advertising, and makes no network requests of its own. Links you tap (for example a component's datasheet URL) open in your browser.

Data collection summary

For the App Store privacy label, the answer to every category is the same: Data Not Collected.
