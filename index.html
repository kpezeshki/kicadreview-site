KiCadReview

Design review for KiCad projects, on iPad.

KiCadReview opens complete KiCad projects — schematics, boards, and the whole sheet hierarchy — as fast Metal-rendered canvases. Cross-probe between board and schematic, highlight nets, measure with snapping, mark up with Apple Pencil, and export review PDFs. Read-only by design: your KiCad files are never modified and never leave the device.

Requires projects saved with KiCad 8 or newer.

Support

Questions, bug reports, or a project that doesn't render right? Email kaveh@pezeshki.org. If a file renders incorrectly, a screenshot of the "unsupported elements" message (if any) helps a lot.
